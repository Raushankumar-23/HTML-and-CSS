GAYA COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING, GAYA

Gaya College of Engineering (GCE), located in Gaya, Bihar, is a premier government engineering institution offering technical education in various engineering disciplines.

Campus and Infrastructure

Spread over 87 acres, the GCE campus provides well-equipped labs, modern classrooms, library facilities, and hostels for both boys and girls.

Undergraduate Programs (B.Tech)

      (1) Civil Engineering (CE) :

      • Focuses on structural design, construction, and infrastructure.
      (2) Computer Science Engineering :

      • Covers areas like software development, data science, and cybersecurity.
      (3) Electrical & Electronics Engineering :

      • Deals with electricity generation, transmission, and control, as well as electronic circuits.
      (4) Mechanical Engineering :

      • Involves design, analysis, and manufacturing of machines and systems.

Postgraduate Programs (M.Tech)

      (1) Structural Engineering :

      • Advanced studies in structural analysis, design, and construction.
      (2) Very Large-Scale Integration (VLSI) Design:

      • Focuses on the design and implementation of integrated circuits.
      (3) Cyber Security :

      • Studies in protecting computer systems and networks from threats.
      (4) Manufacturing Engineering :

      • Deals with the processes and techniques used in the production of goods.

Other Program

      Bachelor of Architecture (B.Arch) :

      • A professional program for aspiring architects.

Admissions

Admissions are through the BCECE Board based on JEE Main ranks, following the government norms and reservation policies.

Placement and Career Opportunities

The Training and Placement Cell works actively to bring companies from various sectors, offering internships and job placements to eligible students.

Student Life and Extracurricular Activities

Students participate in cultural events, tech fests, hackathons, and social outreach programs that contribute to holistic development.

Conclusion

Gaya College of Engineering stands as a growing hub for engineering aspirants in Bihar, fostering innovation, learning, and leadership.
